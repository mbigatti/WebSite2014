60mins

Minimal countdown timer, maximum 60 minutes. Drag or tap the knob to set time, push central button to start / pause. Tap the knob to stop. Swipe up to reveal options.

  • Caffeine mode keeps iPhone up so you can see the countdown (require connecting the power supply or less than 5 minute timer);
  • Choose between two themes: day or night;
  • Tap the megaphone to spread the love!
